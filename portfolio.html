CC4: Replication

Task: Find a published chart, replicate it in Vega-Lite, then improve upon the original design.

Original Chart

Source: Financial Times

Replication

Source: Data traced from Financial Times original chart

Improvements Made:

  • Combined visualisation: Combined 7 separate small multiples into a single unified line chart, significantly improving data-to-ink ratio and enabling direct comparison across banks.
  • Continuous axes: Transformed discrete axis continuous series, revealing full trends more effectively.
  • Improved colour differentiation: Replaced Financial Times' limited dual-colour scheme with distinct colours for each bank, making individual performance immediately identifiable.
  • Added zero baseline reference: Included prominent dotted line at zero in net income chart, making losses and profitability visually obvious at a glance.
  • Clearer titles: Made chart purpose explicit with descriptive titles instead of vague headlines.
