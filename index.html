About Me

Hi, My name is Krisha! I was born in Pune, India and now live in London, pursuing an MPA in Data Science for Public Policy at the London School of Economics and Political Science.

My work revolves around data visualisation, machine learning, climate modelling and nature degradation. I work at the Centre for Economic Transition Expertise (CETEx), assisting the Nature and the Monetary and Fiscal Markets team. At the moment, I am working on a bibliometric network analysis and bank sustainability projects.

Through LSE's PP434 course in Automated Data Visualisation, I created a portfolio and a Nature Degradation and Climate Vulnerability in India project. I developed technical skills in API integration, web scraping, geospatial mapping, machine learning, and interactive dashboard design, all applied to real-world sustainability challenges.
