About Me

I'm a data science student passionate about using visualisation and analytics to understand complex sustainability challenges. My work focuses on the intersection of environmental data and policy, exploring how data-driven insights can inform climate action and sustainable development.

Through automated visualisation techniques and machine learning, I transform raw data into compelling narratives that bridge the gap between technical analysis and accessible communication. My portfolio showcases projects spanning climate vulnerability, nature degradation, and data-driven policy analysis.

Currently completing PP434 - Automated Data Visualisation, I specialise in creating elegant, interactive visualisations using Vega-Lite, Python, and modern web technologies.
